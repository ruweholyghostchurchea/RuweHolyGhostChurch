Support Our Mission & Vision

‘Singruok Tek’ (Sacrifice is strenuous)

Ruwe Holy Ghost Church of East Africa is built on a legacy of sacrifice. Your support allows us to continue our mission to spread the Gospel, heal the sick, and fulfill the prophecies of our founders.

Your Support Builds Our Future

Your generous giving directly funds our key objectives:

The 12-Door Archcathedral

Construction of our new Archcathedral at Ruwe, as prophesied by Lawi Obonyo. This sacred building will stand as a testament to our faith and a beacon for generations to come.

Heritage Preservation

The digitization of our unique Dhoroho hymns, oral histories, and liturgical texts for future generations. Preserving our sacred traditions is vital to our identity.

Education & Empowerment

Community-based social programs focused on Education and Youth/Women's empowerment, building a self-reliant and educated community.

Church Expansion

Establishing new Dioceses in urban centers across East Africa and beyond to serve our migrating congregants and spread the Gospel.

Roho/Dhoroho Learning

Developing standardized curricula for Roho/Dhoroho learning and Bible study in all Dioceses, ensuring our unique teachings are passed down faithfully.
