Our Most Sacred Event: The Musanda Commemoration

The most important event in our liturgical year is the annual commemoration of the Musanda martyrs.

When

Mid-January - Every Year

Commemorating the events of January 16th and January 20-21, 1934

What

Annual remembrance services commemorating the martyrs of Musanda. A central part of this event is the reenactment of the January 16th flag-raising ceremony.

Why

These rites function to sacralize our history and maintain our collective memory across generations.

We invite all members and friends to join us at Ruwe headquarters for this powerful time of remembrance and worship.
