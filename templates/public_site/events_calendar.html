Our Most Sacred Event: The Musanda Commemoration

The most important event in our liturgical year is the annual commemoration of the Musanda martyrs.

When

Mid-January - Every Year

Commemorating the events of January 16th and January 20-21, 1934

What

Annual remembrance services commemorating the martyrs of Musanda. A central part of this event is the reenactment of the January 16th flag-raising ceremony.

Why

These rites function to sacralize our history and maintain our collective memory across generations.

We invite all members and friends to join us at Ruwe headquarters for this powerful time of remembrance and worship.

Additional Church Events

Communal Fasting

Regular fasting days throughout the year, preceding major events to seek the Spirit's favor and prophetic clarity.

Healing Services

Special healing and prophetic ministry services featuring prayer for the sick, exorcism, and spiritual guidance.

Youth & Women's Programs

Educational and empowerment programs for youth and women, building community and strengthening faith.

Church Meetings

Regular gatherings of Church members are held across various churches to plan “Church Events”, update “Church Rules”, solve araising matters, strengthen unity, and spiritual growth.The meetings are usually headed by the Church's Senior Teacher and the Church Office.

Pastorate Meetings

Occasionally, Pastorate Meetings are held across various pastorates to plan “Pastorate Events”, update “Pastorate Rules”, solve araising matters,strengthen Churches collaboration. The meetings are usually headed by the Bishop and the Diocese Office.

Diocese Meetings

From time to time, Diocese Meetings are organized in different dioceses to promote shared fellowship and participation. The meetings are usually headed by the Bishop and the Diocese Office. Lastly, in the case of Archdiocese Meeting... “Holy Synod” is always preferred. The Holy Synod is usually headed by the Archbishop, the King, and the Management Office.
